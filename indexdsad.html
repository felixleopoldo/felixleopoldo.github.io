RESEARCH AREAS:

  • EVIDENTIAL REASONING
    (A. Darwiche) 
    Distributed interpretation of multi-source data in networks of partial beliefs
  • LEARNING
    (J. Pearl, J. Tian) 
    Structuring causal networks to form a representation consistent with theories, experiments, and  observations
  • CONSTRAINT PROCESSING
    (R. Dechter, J. Pearl) 
    Intelligent backtracking, learning while searching, restructuring constraint networks, truth maintenance systems, temporal reasoning
  • GRAPHOIDS
    (J. Pearl) 
    Characterization of informational dependencies and their graph representations
  • CAUSAL REASONING
    (A. Darwiche, J. Pearl, J. Tian, B. Bonet, C. Brito, M. Hopkins) 
    Predicting the effects of actions and policies, identifying causes, and generating explanations for observed events in specific scenarios
