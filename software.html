"Benchpress", A Snakemake workflow for running and comparing structure learning algorithms for probabilistic graphical models.

Some years ago I found myself writing yet another script for benchmarking structure learning algorithms. After I while I got tired of that so I, in collaboration with Jack Kuipers and Giusi Moffa, created Benchpress instead, a general Snakemake workflow which amis to solve the benchmarking problem once and for all. Thanks to Mohamad Elmasri, James Cussens, Alex Markham, and others for your contributions and feedback.
